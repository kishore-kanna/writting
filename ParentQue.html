Screening Test

1. Stutters while speaking?

2. Mispronounces word while speaking?

3. Gets confused in naming objects? (Eg. Table for chair)

4. Asks for repetition again and again when listening to others?

5. Gets confused when many instructions are given at once?

6. Has difficulty in listening to what I say in crowded places?

7. Has difficulty reading?

8. Has difficulty in understanding what other people say?

9. Misses lines or words while reading?

10. Has difficulty in reading aloud?

11. Has difficulty writing?

12. Holds pen, pencil appropriately?

13. Has errors in number identification? (Eg. 3 as 9)

14. Does not maintain left to right orientation?

15. Has difficulty in finger counting?
